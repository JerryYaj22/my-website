Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our website
support and maintenance services.

How much does a website cost?

Website pricing varies based on the scope, features, and complexity of your project. You can click the View Package tab for more information about the prices.

How long does it take to build a website?

Depending on the project size and requirements it can vary. You can click the View Package to see the approximate timeline of each various project.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Yes. Every website we build is fully responsive and optimized for desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Do you offer website maintenance and support?

We provide ongoing website updates, maintenance, and support to keep your site running smoothly. You can click the View Package tab to see our 30 day Website Support.

Can I update my website myself?

Absolutely. We can build your site so you can easily update content, images, and other information when needed.

Do you build eCommerce websites?

Yes. We create online stores with secure payment processing, product management, and shopping cart functionality.

Will my website be optimized for search engines?

Yes. We follow SEO best practices to help improve your website's visibility on search engines.

How do payments work?

We send you a FREE template and if you like it, you can pay the non-refundable 50% deposit which is required before work begins, with the remaining balance due upon project completion.

Is the deposit refundable?

No. Deposits are non-refundable as they secure your project slot and cover initial design and development work.

How do I get started?

Click the Start Your Journey tab and simply contact us with your project details, and we'll discuss your goals, timeline, and website requirements.
